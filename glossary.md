# Real Estate Metrics Glossary
### For CBRE Market Intelligence Dashboard

A plain-language reference for the metrics used in this dashboard.

---

## Leasing Metrics

| Term | Plain Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Total Leased Area** | Total office space rented out in sq ft during the period |
| **Leasing Volume** | Number of lease deals signed |
| **Corridor** | A major commercial zone or micro-market (e.g., Cyber City, Golf Course Road in Gurugram) |
| **Absorption** | How much space is being actively taken up by tenants |

---

## Vacancy & Rental

| Term | Plain Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Vacancy Rate** | % of total available office stock that is currently empty and unleased |
| **Rental Value** | Average monthly rent per sq ft in that market |
| **Vacancy Compression** | When vacancy rate drops over time — a positive signal (less empty space) |
| **Rental Appreciation** | When rental rates rise — signals demand exceeding supply |

---

## Supply & Demand

| Term | Plain Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Supply (sq ft)** | Total office space available (existing + new completions) |
| **Demand (sq ft)** | Estimated space requirement from occupiers |
| **Demand Supply Gap** | Demand minus Supply. Negative = oversupply (more space than takers) |
| **MSF** | Million Sq Ft — a unit used to express large real estate quantities |
| **Demand Supply Gap (000 sq ft)** | Gap converted to thousands sq ft for readability |

---

## Risk Signals (Early Warning)

| Term | Plain Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Vacancy Spike** | Sudden jump in empty space — could mean companies leaving |
| **Demand Drop** | Fewer companies looking to rent — market softening signal |
| **Rental Decline** | Rents falling — landlords under pressure to fill space |
| **Signal Intensity** | 1 = minor blip, 5 = serious market stress |

---

## Benchmarks & KPIs

| Term | Plain Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Max Benchmark (100)** | Index reference point representing target leasing level |
| **KPI Card** | A single big number on a dashboard showing one key metric at a glance |
| **BAN (Big A** Number)** | Dashboard design term for oversized metric displays |

---

## Chart Types Used

| Chart | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Bar Chart** | Compares leasing volume across corridors |
| **Dual-Axis Line** | Tracks two trends (vacancy + rental) on same timeline |
| **Scatter Plot** | Plots supply vs demand to spot imbalance by corridor |
| **Heatmap** | Shows risk intensity across corridors and signal categories |
| **Bullet Chart** | Compares actual leasing against a benchmark |
| **Waterfall Chart** | Shows how vacancy compression accumulates across corridors |
